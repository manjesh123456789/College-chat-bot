Welcome to College Admission Chatbot

Helping students with course details, requirements, and application updates.

Features

User Interaction

Engages users in natural language conversations to guide them through the admission process.

Information Collection

Prompts users to input their name, mobile number, EAMCET rank, interested courses, and intermediate hall ticket number.

Query Handling

Responds to inquiries about course details, admission requirements, application status, and general college information.

User Guidance

Provides step-by-step guidance on how to proceed with their application, including deadlines and necessary documentation.

Feedback Mechanism

Incorporates a feedback mechanism to improve its responses and user experience over time.
